Strength determines your physical attack, defense against physical attack, and ability to preform other abilities which require strength.
Dexterity determines your accuracy, certain aspects of damage with ranged weapons, and ability to preform abilities that require coordination.
Intelligence determines your magic attack, defence against magic attack and spells, and the ability to preform abilities that require mental acuity.
Wisdom determines your proficiency at learning new skills.
Constitution determines your health, mana, stamina, speed of recovery, suceptability to poisons and disease, etc.
Charisma determines your bargaining and persuasion abilities and your amiability.
